Cone Storage Instructions

  • Store cones in the freezer immediately after receiving your order.
  • Cones remain fresh and fully potent for up to 2-3 months when kept frozen.
  • Keep outside at room temperature for 20-30 minutes before starting application.
  • Do not refreeze a cone once it has been fully thawed.
  • Proper freezer storage ensures a vibrant, rich color output.

Before Care (Before Applying)

  • Ensure skin area is completely clean & dry.
  • Avoid applying any oil, lotion, cream, or soap residue.
  • Do not wax or bleach the area within 2 days of application.
  • Good natural body heat helps the stain develop beautifully.
  • Leaving henna on overnight yields the deepest possible colors.

After Care (After Removal)

  • Keep the henna design on for a minimum of 6–8 hours.
  • Scrape paste away gently; do not use water for removal.
  • Soak with coconut or clove oil to loosen dry crust flakes.
  • Avoid water, liquid soaps, or sanitizers for at least 12 hours.
  • The henna stain darkens naturally over the next 24–48 hours.
