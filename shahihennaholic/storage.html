Cone Care & Freshness

  • Store cones directly inside your deep freezer immediately upon arrival.
  • Our organic paste retains high stain quality for up to 2-3 months when frozen properly.
  • Thaw individual cones by placing them at room temperature for 20-30 minutes prior to use.
  • Avoid refreezing any cone structural components once it has been fully thawed.
  • If maintained purely at room temperature, consume contents completely within 24 hours.
  • Correct temperature maintenance ensures premium, uniform, and dark mahogany color processing.
Notice: Since our products are completely organic without chemical additives, temperature management is highly key to maintaining high-grade staining values.
